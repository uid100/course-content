ERD Rubric

Criteria Ratings Pts
File Format and Submission Requirements 10 pts: The ERD is submitted in JPG format, as required.
0 pts: No Marks 		10 pts
Number of Entities (At least 5) 15 pts: 5 or more
10 pts: 4 Entities
5 pts: 3 Entities
0 pts: <3 		15 pts
Correct Identification of Attributes 20 pts: All entities have appropriate attributes.
15 pts: Most entities have correct attributes.
10 pts: Some entities have missing or incorrect attributes.
0 pts: Few or no attributes correctly identified. 		20 pts
Primary Key Identification 15 pts: All primary keys correctly indicated.
10 pts: Most primary keys indicated correctly.
5 pts: Some primary keys missing or incorrectly labeled.
0 pts: No primary keys identified. 		15 pts
Relationship Identification 15 pts: All relationships correctly shown.
10 pts: Some relationships missing or incorrectly drawn.
0 pts: No relationships identified. 		15 pts
Cardinality 15 pts: All relationships have correct cardinality.
10 pts: Some relationships have incorrect or missing cardinality.
0 pts: No cardinality is indicated. 		15 pts
Diagram Clarity and Organization 10 pts: Clear, well-organized diagram.
5 pts: Diagram is somewhat clear but has minor issues with organization.
0 pts: Diagram is cluttered or hard to interpret. 		10 pts
Total Points 100
