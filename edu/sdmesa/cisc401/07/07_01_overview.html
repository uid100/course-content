Module 7 Overview

Data Integration

In this module, we are going to consider the concept of data integration and its importance in the context of healthcare.

Data integration refers to the process of combining data from different sources into a unified view or dataset.

This enables organizations to have access to accurate and comprehensive information. In the healthcare industry, data integration plays a crucial role in improving patient care, enhancing operational efficiency, and enabling better decision-making.

 

As part of this discussion, we are going to be wading through and trying to make sense of some acronym soup.

The healthcare industry is known for its use of acronyms and technical jargon, and data integration is no exception. Some of the key terms and standards you will encounter in this module include Health Information Exchange (HIE), Health Level 7 (HL7), and Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources (FHIR).
