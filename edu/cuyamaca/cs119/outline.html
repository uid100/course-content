Semester
This course will focus on standard programming constructs and patterns. It is intended to apply to any object-oriented programming language, but will use Python as the primary language for examples and assignments.
Students should expect to spend 10-15 hours per week on this course.
The course outline will loosely follow the sequence of topics in the textbook.
