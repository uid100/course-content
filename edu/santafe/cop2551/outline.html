Semester
This course will focus on object-oriented programming constructs and patterns and the C# language and environment.
Students should expect to spend 10-15 hours per week on this course.
Topics
Week 1
module 1. .NET/C# & Data types, Variables, and Operators
Week 2
module 2. Methods and Scope
Week 3
module 3. Control Flow - Decision Making
Week 4
module 4. Control Flow - Loops
Week 5
module 5. Exception Handling
Week 6
module 6. Introduction to Object-Oriented Programming (OOP)
Week 7
module 7. Value types, references, enums & structs
Week 8
module 8. Working with Arrays and Collections (Introduction)
Week 9
module 9. Inheritance
Week 10
module 10. Polymorphism and Interfaces
Week 11
module 11. Properties and fields, Encapsulation and Access Modifiers
Week 12
module 12. wrap-up and final exam
Pattern and due dates
The assignments within each module are typically due on Saturday night at 1159P. Assignments may have 1 or 2 week deadlines.
The details of all the due dates can be found at the bottom of the syllabus page (follow the Canvas link), as well as on the course calendar within Canvas.
