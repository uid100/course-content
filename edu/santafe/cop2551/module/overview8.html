2 Chapters this week

Once again we have two chapters to cover this week. The first is about arrays and it will be the primary focus of this module. The second chapter covers parameter arrays and the params keyword, which is useful, but not as commonly used.

In the Canvas material for this module you will find some review material about variables and some background information about arrays. I recommend that you start with this material. It is brief, but it is helpful. Having a good understanding of variables is essential to understanding arrays and it will help you understand the material in the chapter.

The chapter notes are also included and follow (rougly) the content in the textbook. They are not a substitute for reading the chapter, but they do provide a good overview of the material.

Of course, there is another chapter quiz. Again, I emphasized the array chapter over the content about parameter arrays, but there are some questions about parameter arrays in the quiz. I do recommend that you read the chapter on parameter arrays, but you'll want to spend your time and focus on arrays.

Finally, the lab. Again, the focus is on arrays. These are new exercises this week, so please read the instructions carefully. The lab is designed to help you practice the concepts from the chapter and the chapter notes.
