(from the College Course Outline)
"This course is the capstone course for the Bachelor of Science in Technology Project Management program. Students will work in teams to complete a project for a real-world client. The project will be managed using the principles and practices of project management. The course will include a review of the project management body of knowledge, the development of a project plan, and the execution and control of the project. The course will also include a review of the principles of software engineering and the development of a software project."
