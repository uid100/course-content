module 1 TDD RSI Communications Plan
Activity Steps Facilitation / Planned Activities Considerations
Java Unit-test & Test-Driven Development Introduction Provide an overview of the module and its objectives including an overview of Test-Driven Development (TDD) and the JUnit test framework. pre-requisite knowledge will include basic knowledge of computer programming languages and concepts, but this may be the first exposure to using Eclipse and JUnit testing, which will be required knowledge for all the remaining exercises in the rest of the course.
Engagement Provide step-by-step video to guide students through building a Java project in Eclipse and using the JUnit test framework. Students will need to follow along with the video and complete the exercises in their own Eclipse environment. They will need to have Eclipse and JUnit installed on their computers. A reminder will be included in the weekly announcement to ensure students have are engaging with the 24/7 Discord channel for help, and the weekly office hours.
Also remind and commend students for their recent participation in the Discord channel discussions, as a gentle, and additional reminder to students who may not have been participating.
Updates Mid-week: post screenshots of working lab to Discord, and some commentary about the build. Add a screenshot of the discussion to the announcement as an additional reminder to students to participate in the Discord channel.
Feedback Code review each submission. The Rubric is intended to guide the feedback process. Additional feedback will be provided, directly related to the project and additional comments directed toward coding best-practices intended to make "good" code "better". Collect summary of common issues and successes to share with the class. This will be shared in the weekly announcement.
Conclusion Gather and share general observations and experiences. Often focused feedback may have been provided for particular student submissions, but may benefit others in the class. This will be shared in the weekly announcement.
