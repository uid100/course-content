By the end of the course students will be able to:

  • Select appropriate techniques to tackle and solve problems in the discipline of information security management.
  • Demonstrate an understanding of security and risk management.
  • Create information security management systems planning, documentation, and implementation.
  • Comprehend the history of computer security and how it evolved into information security.
  • Identify key terms and concepts of information security.
  • Outline the phases of the security systems development life cycle.
  • Understand the roles of professionals involved in information security within an organization.
